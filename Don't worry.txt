Don’t worry about the things
I know – and I wish you knew
how much I love you

Don’t worry about the things
I know – and I wish you knew
what remains true

How many times have you lost your smile
when life never said “I’m sorry”?
How many nights did you cry alone
thinking no one saw your worry?

Don’t worry about the things
don’t worry about the pain
I know – and I wish you knew
my love will still remain

Don’t worry about the things
even if the road bends
the truth is in your hands
I’ll love you till the end

If the night comes back again
if your smile fades away
remember this, remember me
I’m not leaving – I will stay

Don’t worry about the things…
my love will still remain…
whatever the ending…
I will still love you.
